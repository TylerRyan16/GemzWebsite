Overview

Gemz is an interplanetary factory-simulation game where players explore and mine alien planets for precious gems. As they refine and cut the gems, players must balance resource extraction with sustainability, navigating ethical dilemmas and environmental consequences. Inspired by games like Factorio and Shapez 2, Gemz challenges players to expand their gem production empire while trading with alien factions and managing the complex economy. The game blends strategic resource management, automation, and environmental ethics, creating an immersive experience where the pursuit of profit comes at a moral cost.
